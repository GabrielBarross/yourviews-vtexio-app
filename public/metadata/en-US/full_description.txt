### About
Increase your conversion rate, estimulate repurchase, earn the trust from your customers and improve your organic search by using reviews.

### Functionalities
#### Store Reviews + NPS
What do you customers think about your e-commerce? How do they evaluate the purchase process, customer service and delivery? Know better your weakness in the customer perspective and gain valueable insights to improve your operation.

#### Product Reviews
By using simple and direct question about your products you can understand exactly what your customers desire and want. Besides you improve your opportunities to reach and convert undecided customers.

#### Questions & Answers
Immediately solve questions from your customers and help them finalized their purchase. 


### How to configure
In order to use this app, you need an active account with Yourviews(https://www.yourviews.com.br/).

Your can get the Configuration Keys on Yourviews Admin Panel. Go to Account > Store Code and grab them.
